Homemade Lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna is a classic Italian dish, perfect for family dinners or special occasions. Layers of tender pasta, creamy ricotta, rich tomato sauce, and savory ground meat come together to create a symphony of flavors. This traditional recipe is easy to follow and guarantees a delicious meal that will satisfy everyone at the table. With a little time and care, you can prepare a dish that will be the star of any gathering. Serve it hot, fresh from the oven, for a comforting and hearty experience.

Ingredients

  • 500 g (1 lb) lasagna sheets (fresh or precooked)
  • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef or pork
  • 700 ml (3 cups) tomato sauce
  • 250 g (1 cup) ricotta cheese
  • 200 g (2 cups) mozzarella cheese (shredded or sliced)
  • 100 g (1 cup) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until soft and fragrant. Add the ground meat and cook until browned. Stir in the tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer for 20-30 minutes over low heat.
  2. Make the ricotta mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with a pinch of nutmeg (if desired), salt, and pepper. For extra flavor, you can also mix in a bit of grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by more meat sauce, a layer of ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a top layer of meat sauce and cheese.
  4. Bake the lasagna: Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbling.
  5. Rest and serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for a final touch, and enjoy!
